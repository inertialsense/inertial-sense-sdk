rbCompute the range and bearing by principle of the osculating sphere: We define a sphere that is tangential to the WGS84 ellipsoid at latitude 1 (the latitude given in lla1). Then we perform the range and bearing computation on that sphere instead of on the ellipsoid itself (which requires more complex math).
